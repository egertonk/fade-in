Musical Chairs

Everyone in the room claims a number. Start the music, the avatars circle the chairs — when you stop it, whoever misses a chair is out. Last one seated wins the contest!

How to play

Share your screen. Each person calls a number (1, 2, 3…). Hit Start music → avatars run around the chairs → hit Stop the music and one avatar is knocked out at random. A chair disappears each round until one champion is left. 🏆
