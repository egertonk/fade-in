Guess the Cocktail

Share your screen — a drink appears, the team shouts its name, you hit Reveal.

How to play

Every drink is drawn in pure code (no pictures): the glass, its colour and a garnish. Show it, let the team call it out, then reveal. Hit Hint for the base spirit if they're stuck.

Tall glass, minty green, lime + mint → Mojito
