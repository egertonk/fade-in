Trivia Game Show

A Jeopardy-style trivia showdown for your team. Share your screen, pick tiles, read the clues aloud, and let the fastest brains win the points.

Your teams

Add 2–4 teams. Names default to “Team 1/2/3”. Use the 🔊 toggle (top-right) to mute all sound — audio only starts once you click something.

How to play

  1. Pick a tile (e.g. Movies for 400) → read the clue aloud.
  2. The team that shouts the right answer gets the points — you tap their name to award it.
  3. Clear the board, then Round 2 doubles the values.
  4. Finish with a Final round where teams wager their points on one last clue.
