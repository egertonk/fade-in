Share your screen — two big accounts appear side by side. The team shouts which one has MORE followers, then you hit Reveal.

How to play

Two real public accounts go head to head. The room shouts A or B — whoever they think has more followers or subscribers. Reveal lights up the winner in gold with ballpark counts, then Next serves up a fresh face-off. Numbers are rough and rounded, so go with your gut.
