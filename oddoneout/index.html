Odd One Out

Share your screen — four things appear, the team argues which one doesn't belong, then you reveal the answer and why.

How to play

Four items show up together; the group debates which one is the odd one out, and the host reveals the correct answer with a single clear reason.

🍎 Apple · 🍌 Banana · 🥕 Carrot · 🍒 Cherry → 🥕 Carrot (it's a vegetable — the rest are fruit)
