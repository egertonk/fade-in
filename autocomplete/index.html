Autocomplete Me

Share your screen — a half-typed search appears, the team shouts what they think the TOP suggestions are, then you hit Reveal.

How to play

A search-bar starter pops up. The whole room shouts what the top autocomplete suggestions would be — the closest or funniest guess wins. Then reveal the "top suggestions" and move to the next one.

"why does my cat…" → guess the top 3 → stare at me while I sleep · knock things off the table · ignore the $40 bed
"how to tell my boss…" → guess the top 3 → I'm working from home · I quit · the meeting could've been an email
