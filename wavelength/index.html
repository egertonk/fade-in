Wavelength

Read your team's mind. One psychic, one word, one dial — get as close to the hidden target as you can.

How to play
  1. One player is the PSYCHIC — everyone else looks away while they peek at where the hidden target sits on the spectrum.
  2. The psychic says ONE word as a clue.
  3. The team then turns the dial to where they think the target is.
  4. Closer to the bullseye = more points (4 / 3 / 2 / 0).
