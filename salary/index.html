Guess the 💰 Salary

Share your screen — a job appears, the team guesses the average salary, you hit Reveal. Closest guess wins.

How to play

A job pops up on screen; everyone shouts out their best guess for the average annual salary, then the host reveals the real figure — closest wins the round.

👨‍✈️ Airline pilot → ~$170,000/yr 🚒 Firefighter → ~$55,000/yr

Figures are approximate average US annual salaries — just for fun.
