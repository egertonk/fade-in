Who Would Say That?

Share your screen — read the line, the team shouts WHO would say it, you hit Reveal.

How to play

A classic thing a grown-up says to a kid pops up — the team shouts who in the family (or beyond) would most likely say it, then you reveal the answer.

"Wait till your father gets home!" → Mom
"Back in my day, we walked 10 miles to school — uphill both ways." → Grandpa
